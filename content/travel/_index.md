## Getting to Asheville

If you plan to fly, we highly recommend Asheville Regional Airport (AVL), which is about 20 minutes from downtown Asheville. The next closest option is the Greenville-Spartanburg International Airport (GSP), which is about 1 hour and 15 minutes from Asheville.
